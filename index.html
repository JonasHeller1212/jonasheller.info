01 / About

Bridging technology
and human behavior

I am an Assistant Professor of Marketing at the School of Business and Economics, Maastricht University. My research mission is to generate evidence-based insights about how emerging technologies impact consumers and societies.

I hold a PhD in Marketing from the University of New South Wales (2019), which was awarded the Dean's Award for Outstanding PhD Theses. Before joining Maastricht University, I worked as a Post-Doctoral researcher at the Brightlands Institute for Smart Society (BISS).

My work sits at the intersection of digital technologies and sensory marketing — investigating how augmented reality, virtual reality, and AI shape the way people experience products, make decisions, and interact with brands.
