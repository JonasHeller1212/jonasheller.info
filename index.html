About Jonas Heller

Jonas Heller is an Assistant Professor at Maastricht University, School of Business and Economics (since 2020), with over 10 years of experience in emerging technologies research. He is co-founder and Scientific Director of the DEXLab (Digital Experience Lab) at SBE.

His research focuses on consumer decision-making in emerging technologies, including Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs). He supervises PhD candidates and coordinates courses in Marketing and Supply Chain Management.

Before academia, Jonas worked at Zalando (transport network optimization) and Jimdo, bringing real-world industry perspective to his research and consulting work.
