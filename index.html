About

Academic rigor meets
real-world impact

I'm Jonas Heller — Assistant Professor of Marketing at Maastricht University and a researcher obsessed with how emerging technologies change the way people think, decide, and buy.

With a PhD from the University of New South Wales (awarded the Dean's Award for Outstanding PhD Theses) and a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellowship, I bring deep academic expertise to every engagement. But I don't speak in jargon — I translate research into clear, strategic insights that business leaders can act on immediately.

Whether it's a 3,000-person conference or an intimate boardroom session, I help organizations understand what AI, AR/VR, and immersive tech actually mean for their customers, their teams, and their bottom line.
